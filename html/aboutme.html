How It Started

This is my origin story

Please note that the story below is fictional and does not contain real people or events.

I know how to fly!

When I was a young girl, I was always fascinated with birds. I would spent hours staring at the sky, in awe of the incredible creatures soaring above me. Why could I fly like that? Why weren't humans given wings?

I pondered on the greek myth of Icarus and Daedalus. I don't suppose you've heard of it? In the story, Daedalus and his son, Icarus, are trapped on the island of Crete. In an effort to escape, Daedalus creates wings made from feathers and wax.

Daedalus and Icarus use their wings to fly from their prison and escape. All was going well until Icarus began to fly a little too close to the sun. Daedalus warned his son to fly lower, but the freedom and warmth Icarus felt had too strong of a pull.

The heat from the sun melted the wax holding Icarus' feathers together, and he fell to his death.

This story holds an unfortunate ending. However, my focus landed on the feeling Icarus must have had when he first soared in the air.

I was captivated. So, I became a Daedalus of my own and created wings that would allow me to fly. Only this time, I was going to learn from Daedalus and Icarus.

It was not an easy process, but I eventually succeeded in building the world's first ever human wings.

I was absolutely elated. Finally, I could fly just like Icarus, just like the majestic birds I grew up watching in the sky.

And that is how I wanted to spend the rest of my life.

There will be times when we are faced with seemingly impossible tasks. The only thing we can do is keep going, one step at a time, minute by minute, until we finally reach our goal.

Only then will we find true success.
